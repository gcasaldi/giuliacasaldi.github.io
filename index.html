PhishForge – AI-powered phishing detection

Lightweight multi-layer engine for URL & email phishing risk scoring.

PhishForge analyzes URLs and emails using heuristics, threat-intelligence checks and a versioned machine learning model. It outputs a 0–100 risk score with an explainable breakdown of suspicious indicators, designed for real-world phishing scenarios.

Tech: Python, FastAPI, Scikit-learn, TF-IDF, Logistic Regression
